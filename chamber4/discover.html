Rexburg Main Street

Main Street is home to many local businesses, which are the lifeblood of this wonderful city.

Historic Downtown

Founded in 1883, Rexburg has over a century of rich history, including many classic buildings downtown.

Growing Neighborhoods

With so many people wanting to live here, Rexburg has a quickly growing population and developing housing to match it.

Brigham Young University - Idaho

Rexburg is proud to be home of one of the fastest growing Universities in the Country, BYU-I.

Rexburg LDS Temple

A beautiful building, it holds on of the best views of the city.

Entertainment

Located in beautiful Eastern Idaho, there are plenty of unique attractions just a short drive away.

Food

Rexburg boasts a wide variety of local and foreign cuisine, offering plenty of options for eating out.

Parks

Parks are one of East Idaho's specialties, as any true Idahoan loves the outdoors.

Events

Including Hemming Village, Rexburg has dozens of organizations hosting and sponsoring new and traditional community events every year.
