Target Audience

Audience Description

The target audience for the Mud Lake Chamber of Commerce is the business owners and community members in the local communities of Terreton, Mud Lake, Howe, Roberts, Hamer, and Dubois.

Personas

Spencer

Spencer lives on his family farm with his wonderful wife and three kids. He raises cattle, and harvests alfalfa. He's 33, has grown up and lived in Hamer most of his life, and learned how to work hard from his father. Spencer loves farming and really enjoys the community he's in. He recently inherited some grazing pasture from his father, but doesn't have enough cattle to graze it all. He also needs to buy some hay to feed his cattle, as he is starting to plant a new crop this year. So, Spencer has a few business ventures he would like to take.

Hailey

Hailey just moved to Terreton with her new husband, to be closer to his family. She's 28 years old, is from St. George, Utah, loves the outdoors, and is excited to raiser her new family inv the country. They just bought a new house and are expecting a baby early next year. Her husband just got a job at Ron's Tire shop and Hailey needs something to do with her time. She loved gardening with her mom as a kid, so she decides to start a new garden in the back yard, planting fruits and vegetables. She also builds a chicken coop. Once those have been properly set up, she starts on another side project, crafting stylish house decorations and gardening accessories.

Scenarios

Spencer has plenty of grazing pasture he inherited, but doesn't have cattle for it. He would love a place to easily, publicly announce his offer with the details.

Spencer also has cattle he needs to feed. He is just planting a new alfalfa field, but it won't produce until next year. He needs to put an ad somewhere so he can buy.

Hailey is new to the community and wants to make new friends. She needs somewhere she can go to learn about community events to attend to get to know the community better.

Hailey is starting garden and a chicken coop in her backyard. She wants to start selling fresh fruits, vegetables, and chicken eggs to members of the community. She has social media pages, but wants somewhere else for exposure to get an extra bump.

Hailey also wants to start an arts & crafts business, building home decorations and gardening accessories. She wants to start an arts & crafts community events, but needs somewhere to help her out with the details.
